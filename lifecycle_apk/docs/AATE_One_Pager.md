# AATE — One Pager

## The Pitch
**AATE (Autonomous Algorithmic Trading Engine)** is the world's most advanced open-source Solana trading bot. Built in under 7 days with 63,000+ lines of production Kotlin code, featuring a 12-layer AI consensus system that learns from every trade.

---

## The Problem
- Manual crypto trading is emotionally exhausting and time-consuming
- Existing bots are either too simple (single indicator) or black-box (no transparency)
- No shared learning across trading instances
- High barrier to entry for algorithmic trading

## The Solution
A self-learning Android trading bot that:
- Makes decisions through **12 AI layers** working in consensus
- Supports **18 specialized trading modes** for any market condition
- **Learns continuously** from every trade outcome
- **Shares learnings** across all instances via Turso Collective Intelligence

## Technology Stack

- **Platform**: Native Android (Kotlin)
- **AI**: Multi-layer consensus with continuous learning
- **Blockchain**: Solana (Jupiter, Helius, DexScreener)
- **Database**: Local encrypted + Turso (collective sync)
- **Security**: AES-256, Hardware Keystore, Jito MEV protection
