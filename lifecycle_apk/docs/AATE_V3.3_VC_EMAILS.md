## INVESTOR FAQ

**Q: Why native Android vs web?**
A: Performance. Trading requires millisecond decisions. Native app is faster, works offline, and provides better UX.

**Q: How do 24 AI layers work together?**
A: Each layer scores one aspect (liquidity, momentum, whales, exits, etc.). MetaCognitionAI weighs their inputs, FluidLearningAI adjusts thresholds, SellOptimizationAI handles exits.

**Q: What's FluidLearning?**
A: Day 1 the bot uses loose thresholds (30% confidence) so it can trade immediately. As it accumulates trades and learns, thresholds tighten to 75-80%. No cold start problem.

**Q: What's the moat?**
A: 1) 24 layers took 18+ months to build. 2) Fluid Learning and Collective Intelligence create compounding advantages. 3) SellOptimization is patent-worthy exit strategy tech.

**Q: What about regulations?**
A: We're a tool, not a financial advisor. Users control their own wallets. We never touch their funds.
