## OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Week 1: Warm Network
- Personal connections
- LinkedIn 2nd-degree intros
- Twitter DMs to angels

### Week 2: Crypto-Native VCs
- Solana-focused funds first
- Trading/DeFi specialists
- Include demo video

### Week 3: AI/Fintech VCs
- Emphasize 25 AI layers
- Position as "AI-first trading"
- Highlight execution story

### Week 4: Media & Influencers
- Pitch the story, not just product
- "Built in 8 days on a phone" angle
- Offer exclusive first look
