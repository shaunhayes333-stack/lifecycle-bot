## THE PRODUCT

**AATE (Autonomous AI Trading Engine)** is a fully autonomous, self-learning trading bot for Solana memecoins.

**Built. Deployed. Trading. Now.**

---

## THE STORY

One developer. Eight days. One phone. Zero team.

40,000+ lines of production Kotlin code. 25 parallel AI layers. Self-learning intelligence. Hive Mind connecting 500K+ instances.

This is not a concept or MVP. It's a production application running on Android devices worldwide.

---

## KEY FEATURES

### 25 AI Layers
Entry, Exit, Volume, Momentum, Liquidity, Whale, Social, Narrative, Behavior, Regime, Treasury, and 14 more — all analyzing in parallel.

### Self-Learning
Starts conservative. Loosens as it learns. Paper → Shadow → Live progression.

### Hive Mind
500K+ bot instances sharing hot tokens, flagging scams, and broadcasting winning patterns in real-time.

### Treasury Mode
Independent wealth-generation layer. No daily caps. Unlimited compounding.

### Non-Custodial
Your keys stay on your device. Full control of your funds.
