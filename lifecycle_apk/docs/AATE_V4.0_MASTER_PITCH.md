## WHAT IT DOES

### Autonomous Trading
- Scans 1000+ tokens per minute across DEX Screener, Birdeye, and pump.fun
- 25 AI layers score every token in parallel
- Executes buys/sells automatically based on learned patterns
- Manages positions with dynamic stop-losses and take-profits

### Self-Learning
- Starts conservative, loosens thresholds as it learns
- Paper trading builds confidence before risking capital
- Shadow trading validates strategies with zero exposure
- Live trading refines the model with real market feedback

### Treasury Mode (Wealth Generation)
- Independent scalping layer separate from main trading
- No daily profit caps—runs unlimited
- Compounds profits automatically into larger positions
- Targets high-probability micro-gains

### Hive Mind (Swarm Intelligence)
- Every bot instance connects to collective network
- Hot tokens broadcast across all instances in real-time
- Scam contracts flagged network-wide instantly
- Big wins/losses shared to accelerate collective learning

### Multi-Currency Support
- Display balances in 23 currencies
- SOL, USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, JPY, CAD, CHF, CNY, HKD, SGD, NZD, SEK, NOK, DKK, INR, BRL, MXN, ZAR, KRW, TRY, BTC, ETH
